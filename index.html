QUEER FIGHT
NIGHT

@ OLIVE'S

📅 Every Tuesday 🕕 6 PM – 10 PM 📍 Cap Hill, Seattle

Queer Fight Night is a weekly fighting game social, every Tuesday night in Capitol Hill, Seattle.

The event is free, and open to anyone regardless of identity — or skill level! Feel free to come just to socialize, this isn't a tournament, you don't have to be good.

We mostly play Rivals of Aether, Melee, Ultimate, and Guilty Gear, but we can set up any game upon request!

We cook food every week for all in attendance. Snacks, drinks, extra controllers, or extra setups are always appreciated. If you can't bring anything, but would still like to help out, we have a KoFi page where you can support what we do!

QUEER GAME NIGHT

🗓 Every Thursday 📍 Cap Hill, Seattle 🕕 6 PM – 12 PM

Queer Game Night is a weekly MTG & board game night, every Thursday in Capitol Hill, Seattle.

We play a lot of Commander, and there's always a deck to borrow! We also play Cube Draft and other board games if people are feeling up for them!

Snacks, extra decks, and extra dice/playmats for newcomers are always appreciated!
